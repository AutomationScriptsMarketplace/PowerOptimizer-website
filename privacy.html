Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2025

Introduction

PowerOptimizer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our macOS application PowerOptimizer (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

Battery and System Data

The App collects the following information locally on your device:

  • Battery charge percentage and capacity
  • Battery health metrics and temperature
  • Charge/discharge cycles
  • Power usage statistics
  • Voltage and amperage readings
  • System hardware identifiers (for battery monitoring only)

Important: All battery and system data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

iCloud Sync Data (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your battery history and statistics are encrypted and synced via Apple's iCloud service
  • App preferences and settings are synced across your devices
  • This data is protected by Apple's iCloud security and encryption

Data We Do NOT Collect

PowerOptimizer does NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Browsing history or app usage outside of PowerOptimizer
  • Marketing or advertising identifiers
  • Any data for third-party analytics or advertising

How We Use Your Information

The information collected by PowerOptimizer is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying real-time battery statistics and health metrics
  • Generating personalized optimization recommendations
  • Creating historical charts and trends
  • Providing predictive analytics about battery health
  • Sending local notifications about battery events (if enabled)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All data collected by PowerOptimizer is stored locally on your Mac using Core Data with the following security measures:

  • Data is stored in your user's Application Support directory
  • Files are protected by macOS file system permissions
  • No data leaves your device unless you enable iCloud sync

iCloud Storage (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's iCloud
  • Synchronization uses CloudKit with end-to-end encryption
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your battery data remains on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

We may disclose information only in the following situations:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with applicable laws or legal processes
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

Third-Party Services

PowerOptimizer uses the following Apple services:

  • CloudKit: For optional iCloud synchronization (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • IOKit Framework: For accessing battery hardware information

No other third-party services, analytics, or advertising frameworks are used.

Children's Privacy

PowerOptimizer is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not contain content specifically targeted at children.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data collected in the app's interface
  • Deletion: Clear all history using the "Clear History" button in Settings
  • Portability: Export your data via iCloud sync
  • Control: Disable data collection by stopping monitoring
  • Opt-out: Disable iCloud sync or notifications at any time

Data Retention

Battery history data is retained for the period you specify in the app settings (default: 30 days). You can adjust this retention period or manually clear all data at any time. When you uninstall the App, all local data is permanently deleted from your device.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since PowerOptimizer does not collect or sell personal information, these rights are automatically respected.

European Union Privacy Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). PowerOptimizer is designed with privacy by default - we minimize data collection and provide full user control over all data.
