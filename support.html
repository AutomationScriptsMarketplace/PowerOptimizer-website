Support & Help

We're here to help you get the most out of PowerOptimizer

Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I install PowerOptimizer?

A: Download PowerOptimizer from the Mac App Store. Once downloaded, the app will appear in your Applications folder. Launch it and follow the setup instructions.

Q: What are the system requirements?

A: PowerOptimizer requires macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later. It works on both Apple Silicon and Intel-based Macs.

Q: How do I enable menu bar access?

A: On first launch, PowerOptimizer will request permission to add an icon to your menu bar. Click "Allow" when prompted. You can manage this in System Settings > Privacy & Security.

Features & Functionality

Q: How accurate are the battery predictions?

A: Our ML algorithms improve accuracy over time as they learn your usage patterns. Initial predictions may vary but become more accurate after 7-14 days of use.

Q: Why doesn't my battery show 100% health?

A: Battery health naturally degrades over time with use. This is normal. A health percentage above 80% is considered good for most batteries.

Q: What do the optimization recommendations mean?

A: Recommendations are personalized suggestions based on your battery usage patterns. They help extend battery lifespan and improve performance.

Troubleshooting

Q: The app isn't updating battery information

A: Ensure monitoring is enabled in the app settings. Try stopping and restarting monitoring. If the issue persists, restart the app.

Q: iCloud sync isn't working

A: Check that you're signed into iCloud in System Settings and that iCloud Drive is enabled. Also verify that PowerOptimizer has iCloud sync enabled in its settings.

Q: Notifications aren't appearing

A: Check System Settings > Notifications > PowerOptimizer and ensure notifications are allowed. Also verify they're enabled in the app settings.

Data & Privacy

Q: Is my battery data shared with anyone?

A: No. All data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account only.

Q: How do I delete my data?

A: Use the "Clear History" button in Settings to delete all stored battery data. Uninstalling the app also removes all local data.

Q: Can I export my battery data?

A: Currently, data can be synced via iCloud. Direct export functionality is planned for a future update.
